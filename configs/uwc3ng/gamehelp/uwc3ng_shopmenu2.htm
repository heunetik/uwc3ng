Shopmenu2 Items

The following items are available for purchase at the shop (say /shopmenu2)

Scroll of Respawning:

You will respawn after death

Mole Protectant:

Protects you from moles (unless they hit you in the head)

Amulet of the Cat:

You cannot be heard when running/going up ladders

Sock of the Feather:

Allows you to levitate

Helm of Excellence:

Immunity to headshots

Flaming Gloves of Warmth:

Given an HE grenade every 25 seconds

Ring of Regeneration +1:

Gives 1 health every 2 seconds, you can have 5 max (type rings to receive 5)

Chameleon:

You will be disguised as the enemy!!

Mole:

Teleports you to the enemy spawn under disguise at the start of the round
