Skills Information (Page 1)

The following skills are available on the first page:

Levitation (LVL1):

Reduces your gravity allowing you to jump higher.

Equipment Reincarnation (LVL1):
[1: 33% / 2: 67% / 3: 100%]

Gives you a chance to start with weapons/equipment you died with.

Impale (LVL1): [1:7% /+3% Per Level / 10: 34%]

Chance damage causes enemies screen to shake when they are hit.

Devotion Aura (LVL2):

Increases your maximum HP.

Healing Wave (LVL2):

Heals you and nearby teammates a few hp every few seconds.

Siphon Mana (LVL2):

Steals enemies money on damage done.

Leather Skin (LVL2):

Gives you and nearby teammates a few armor every few seconds.

Vampiric Aura (LVL3):

Steal HP back every time you deal damage to an enemy.
