UWC3NG Ultimates

Unlike previous versions of the Warcraft3 and Warcraft Frozen Throne plugins, Ultimate Warcraft3 NG allows players to train in up to 3 ultimate skills.

In order to make use of the ultimate skills in UWC3NG you will need to bind a key for each ultimate and each ultimate has a unique command to be bound. Special abilities are also handled the same way in UWC3NG:

Suicide Bomber (wcsuicide)

Chain Lightning (wclightning)

Entangling Roots (wcentangle)

Big Bad Voodoo (wcvoodoo)

Vengeance (Passive Ability)

Locust Swarm (wclocust)

Gate (wcgate)

Ability: Mend Wounds (wcmend)

Ability: Repair Armor (wcrepair)

Ability: Serpant Ward (wcward)
