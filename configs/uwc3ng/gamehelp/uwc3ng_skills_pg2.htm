Skills Information (Page 2)

The following skills are available on the first page:

Spiked Carapace (LVL3):

Mirrors a percentage of damage to enemies shooting you, and you gain armor.

Repair Armor (LVL4) [bind wcrepair]:

Allows you to restore some of your teammates armor (for a XP Bonus) on button press. Requires a key bound to wcrepair

Mend Wounds (LVL4) [bind wcmend]:

Allows you to restore some of your teammates armor (for a XP Bonus) on button press. Requires a key bound to wcmend

Unholy Aura (LVL5):

Increases your maximum speed. Stacks with agility and boots of speed.

Hex (LVL5):

Chance to turn your enemies into jumping zombies on damage done.

Bash (LVL6):

Chance to freeze enemies on damage done.

Serpent Ward (LVL6) [bind wcward]:

Place wards that cause damage to enemies touching them. Requires a key bound to wcward

Banish (LVL7):

Chance to slap enemies into the air on damage done.
