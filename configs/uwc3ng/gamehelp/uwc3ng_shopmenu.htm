Shopmenu Items

The following items are available for purchase at the shop (say /shopmenu)

Ankh of Reincarnation:

If you die you will retrieve your equipment the following round

Boots of Speed:

Allows you to run faster

Claws of Attack +6:

An additional 6 hp will be removed from the enemy on every hit

Cloak of Shadows:

Makes you partially invisible, stacks with the invisibility skill, and invisibility is increased when holding the knife

Mask of Death:

You will receive health for every hit on the enemy

Necklace of Immunity:

You will be immune to enemy ultimates

Orb of Frost:

Slows your enemy down when you hit him

Periapt of Health:

Receive extra health

Tome of Experience:

Purchasing a tome increases your experience by a fixed amount, the tome is consumed
