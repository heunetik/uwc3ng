Skills Information (Page 5)

Vengeance: (LVL15) [passive ultimate]:

Will respawn with half health you when you die.

Chain Lightning (LVL15) [bind wclightning]:

Calls a bolt of lightning that splits from the target to nearby enemies as well.

Entangle Roots (LVL16) [bind wcentangle]:

Allows you to prevent an enemy player from moving for 10 seconds

Flame Strike (LVL16):

Chance that damage done to an enemy will set him alight with flame.

Suicide Bomber (LVL17):

When you die you will explode damage nearby enemies

Fan of Knives (LVL19):

You have a percentage chance at the start of the next round you will be a mole.

Evasion (LVL24):

Chance that you will evade damage and enemy skills.

Trueshot Aura (LVL24):

Adds a percentage to damage you deal to enemies
