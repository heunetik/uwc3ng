Skills Information (Page 4)

The following skills are available on the third page:

Critical Grenades (LVL12):

Increases the damage done by grenades

Big Bad Voodoo (LVL12) [bind wcvoodoo]:

Makes you immune to damage for 3 seconds

Gate (LVL12) [bind wcgate]:

Chance that any hits damage may be doubled.

Invisibility (LVL13):

Makes you more transparent the higher your level. Stacks with cloak item, more invisible if your knife is out.

Pheonix (LVL13):

Chance that you will revive a fallen teammate

Napalm Grenades (LVL13):

Potential that grenade damage will set enemies on fire.

Siphon Armor (LVL14):

Chance that any hit done to an enemy will steal armor.

Locust Swarm (LVL14) [bind wclocust]:

A Swarm of Locusts attacks the enemy
