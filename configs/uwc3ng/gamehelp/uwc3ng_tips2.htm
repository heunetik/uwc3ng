More UWC3NG Tips

  • Abilities are also bound like Ultimates in UWC3NG and their commands are also displayed next to their skill name in the menu
  • There are 2 shops in UWC3NG where magical items can be bought, these range from items that increase your speed or attack abilities to some that will disguise you like the enemy or allow you to spawn in the enemy camp at the start of a round. More information is available by saying /itemsinfo
  • One skill that is new to UWC3NG is called Mend Wounds and beside being useful to your team by allowing you to heal damage, cure poison and ease burns, it also provides a reliable source of earning experience.
  • You can reset your current skills by saying /resetskills - at the start of the next round your points will be returned and you may retrain a new set of skills. If by slim chance you ever want to delete your skills and XP you can do so with /deletexp
