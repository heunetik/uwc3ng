Skills Information (Page 3)

The following skills are available on the third page:

Thorns Aura: (LVL8):

Inflicts a mirror percentage of damage on enemies firing on you

Teleport (LVL8) [bind wcteleport]:

Allows you to teleport to where your crosshair is aiming.

Critical Strike (LVL9):

Chance that any hits damage may be doubled.

Dispell Hex (LVL9) [bind wcdispell]:

On button press, removes hex from teammates, and awards you an XP Bonus

Carrion Beetles (LVL10):

Chance that you will infect someone with disease when you damage an enemy.

Steel Skin (LVL11):

[Requires 1 level Vampiric Aura and 3 levels Leather Skin] Upgrades your Leather Skin and allows Vampiric Aura to steal armor from your enemy

Shadow Strike (LVL11):

Chance that you will poison when you damage an enemy.

Blink (LVL11):

Chance that you will block enemy ultimates
