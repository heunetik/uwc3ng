UWC3NG Attributes

Strength

Determines your starting base health, stacks with Devotion Aura

Intellect

Each level of intellect you enhance from 11 - 20 provides a different benefit:

11 - Impale Bonus
12 - Siphon Bonus
13 - Banish Bonus
14 - Hex Bonus
15 - Bash Bonus
16 - Trueshot Bonus
17 - Reduce Teleport cooldown
18 - Various (+1 gate)
19 - Locust Swarm Bonus
20 - Vengeance Bonus (+1 respawn)

Dexterity

Helps to successfully Evade shots for characters trained in Evasion

Agility

Determines your base runspeed, stacks with Unholy Aura

Constitution

Provides health regeneration which stacks with Healing Wave
